Changepay


Name : Changepay
Sector : E-commerce and payments
Team Size : 5-10
Team Pitch : Pitch
Description: Changepay is a customizable commerce platform that aims to change the way business and communication conducted in large residential campuses & close-knit communities by empowering local merchants & enterprises to provide delightful service, while ensuring a convenient and seamless experience for the student/consumer. We are leveraging our Smartbox technology to solve problems of residential students and help campus to achieve effective social distancing once the students are back in the hostel as per government recommendation. We are currently live in NITK- Surathkal and other campuses with 19 various category merchants. We have processed more than 40,000+ orders in a span of 6 months. We also work closely with student clubs on various campuses to help them build Apps and business on top of our platform to solve unique campus-related solutions and give them first-hand experience towards entrepreneurship.
